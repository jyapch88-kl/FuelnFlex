# Fuel & Flex Blueprint

## Vision

A unified app that monitors nutrition intake, plans for training in high-intensity events or specific health goals.

## Design and Features

*   **Landing Page:** An inspiring entry point to the app, showcasing success stories and outcomes.
*   **Dashboard:** A central view to track progress towards nutrition and fitness goals.
*   **About Me:** A section for users to input their biodata (height, weight) and see calculated metrics like BMI.
*   **Community:** A space for users to connect, share progress, and support each other.
    *   **Forum Boards:** A forum-style community section with boards for different topics.
    *   **Feature Voting:** A section for users to vote on proposed new features for the app.
*   **AI Health Chat:** A chatbot that connects users to a Doctor, Coach, or Dietitian for personalized advice.
*   **Nutrition Tracking:** A tool to log meals and monitor daily caloric intake.
    *   **Photo Upload:** Users can upload a photo of their food or drink.
    *   **Auto-Calculation:** The app will automatically calculate the calorie intake from the photo.
    *   **Suggestions:** The app will provide suggestions based on the meal.
*   **Fitness Goal Planning:** A feature to set, track, and manage fitness goals.
    *   **Bespoke Plan Generation:** Depending on their goal, a plan will be created that is bespoke to them.
    *   **Custom Goals:** Users can create their own custom goals.
*   **Alert Mechanism:** An alert system to inform users if they are deviating from their goals.
*   **Admin Page:** A separate page for administrators to monitor web traffic and user statistics.
